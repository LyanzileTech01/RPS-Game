How to Play RPS Game

Step 1: Choose your move (Rock, Paper, or Scissor) to beat the bot.

Step 2: Your move and the bot's move are compared to see the winner.

Step 3: If you win, your score increases by +0.5.

Step 4: You can see your chosen move and the bot's move in real-time.

Step 5: Check the scoreboard to track Player vs Bot total wins.

Rules:

  • 🪨 Rock beats Scissor
  • ✂️ Scissor beats Paper
  • 📄 Paper beats Rock
